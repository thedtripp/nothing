Product Manager, Nothing.

Product · Remote · Full-time

As our Product Manager, you'll define the roadmap for a product that must, under all circumstances, remain exactly the same.

What you'll do

  • Say no to every feature request, professionally and in writing
  • Run quarterly planning to reconfirm the roadmap has not changed
  • Conduct user interviews to confirm customers are receiving nothing as expected
  • Partner with Engineering to make sure nothing new gets built

What we're looking for

  • 3+ years of product management experience, ideally on a team that shipped less over time
  • Excellent stakeholder management, particularly around disappointing people gently
  • A deep, personal conviction that less is more, taken to its logical extreme

Nice to have

  • Experience with subscription products; bonus points for a personal subscription you don't use

Compensation

$130,000–$155,000/year base, plus a roadmap you are contractually forbidden from changing.
