Site Reliability Engineer, Absence Infrastructure.

Engineering · Remote · Full-time

You'll be responsible for making sure our infrastructure for delivering nothing never goes down — mostly by making sure it's never turned on in the first place.

What you'll do

  • Monitor dashboards that show no data, and confirm they remain empty
  • Write incident postmortems for incidents that did not occur
  • Own our five-nines uptime SLA for a service with no server
  • Hold a theoretical on-call rotation, never triggered

What we're looking for

  • Experience with monitoring and observability tooling, primarily to confirm nothing is happening
  • Strong written communication, for explaining to stakeholders why there is nothing to report
  • Comfortable with ambiguity, and with the total absence of ambiguity, depending on the day

Nice to have

  • Cloud platform certifications for infrastructure you will not be provisioning

Compensation

$135,000–$160,000/year base, plus a pager that will never go off.
