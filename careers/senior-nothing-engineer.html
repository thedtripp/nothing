Senior Nothing Engineer.

Engineering · Remote · Full-time

Absolutely Nothing's Engineering team is responsible for the design, non-implementation, and continued non-delivery of our core product: nothing. As a Senior Nothing Engineer, you'll own critical decisions about what not to build next.

What you'll do

  • Maintain 100% uptime by ensuring nothing is ever deployed
  • Review pull requests and decline all of them, with kindness
  • Partner with Product to scope the absence of new features
  • Sit on-call for incidents that cannot occur, because nothing runs
  • Write and delete roughly equal amounts of code, netting zero lines shipped per quarter

What we're looking for

  • 5+ years of professional software engineering experience, ideally shipping products with fewer features over time
  • Strong opinions about architecture, and the discipline to never act on them
  • Comfortable operating with no roadmap, no backlog, and no sprint planning
  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field, or equivalent experience doing nothing professionally

Nice to have

  • Experience maintaining legacy systems by simply not touching them
  • Prior experience at an early-stage startup that also failed to ship anything

Compensation

$145,000–$170,000/year base, plus equity in a company most recently valued at $0.
