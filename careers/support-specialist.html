Customer Support Specialist, Tier Zero.

Customer Success · Remote · Full-time

You'll be the first line of support for customers experiencing exactly what was advertised: nothing.

What you'll do

  • Respond to support tickets confirming that yes, this is working as intended
  • Maintain a 100% resolution rate by explaining there is nothing to resolve
  • Escalate to Tier One only in the rare event a customer receives something
  • Write and maintain help-center articles for a product with no features to document

What we're looking for

  • Prior customer support experience, ideally in SaaS
  • Excellent written communication and genuinely infinite patience
  • Able to de-escalate customers who suspect, correctly, that they are paying for nothing

Nice to have

  • Experience with a support helpdesk tool, used here mainly to confirm the queue is empty

Compensation

$48,000–$58,000/year base, plus a support queue that is, technically, always at inbox zero.
