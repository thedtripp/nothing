Compliance Counsel, Nothing-as-a-Service.

Legal & Finance · Remote · Full-time

You'll ensure Absolutely Nothing stays fully compliant while selling, legally and explicitly, nothing at all.

What you'll do

  • Draft and maintain Terms of Service disclosing, in plain language, that customers will receive nothing
  • Review marketing copy for any claim that could be mistaken for an actual product
  • Advise on subscription billing regulations for recurring charges with no deliverable
  • Monitor consumer protection law in jurisdictions where we may eventually sell nothing

What we're looking for

  • JD and active bar membership
  • Experience with consumer protection, subscription billing, or FTC disclosure requirements
  • Comfortable being the only person in the room saying "we should probably disclose that"

Nice to have

  • A sense of humor. You will need it.

Compensation

$160,000–$190,000/year base, plus the professional satisfaction of being right about the disclaimer.
