Content Lead, Corporate Lore.

Marketing · Remote · Full-time

You'll write the ongoing story of Absolutely Nothing — press releases, investor updates, and engineering notes — in a voice so sincere nobody can tell it's a joke.

What you'll do

  • Write monthly company updates announcing milestones that did not happen
  • Maintain brand consistency across a universe of fictional executives
  • Partner with Legal to ensure our press releases remain technically true
  • Draft customer testimonials from customers who are, legally, real people

What we're looking for

  • A portfolio of deadpan or satirical writing
  • Comfortable writing in corporate voice without ever breaking character
  • Background in marketing, journalism, comedy writing, or a demonstrated ability to keep a straight face in print

Nice to have

  • Experience in corporate communications and comedy writing, ideally not at the same job

Compensation

$95,000–$120,000/year base, plus full creative control over press releases nobody fact-checks.
